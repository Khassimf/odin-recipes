Lasagna

Lasagne are a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types, made of very wide, flat sheets. The dish may be topped with grated cheese, which becomes melted after baking. Typically, cooked pasta is assembled with other ingredients and then baked in the oven. The resulting casserole is cut into single serving square portions. The main ingredients of Lasagna are meat, cheese, cream, lasagna pasta.

Ingredients

  • Tomato sauce
  • Beef
  • Lasagna sheet
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Mozzarella
  • Garlic
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Start by making the sauce with ground beef, bell peppers, onions and a combo of tomato sauce, tomato paste and crushed tomatoes. The three kinds of tomatoes gives the sauce great depth of flavor.
  2. Let this simmer while you boil the noodles and get the cheese ready. We're using ricotta, shredded Mozzarella, and Parmesan.
  3. From there, it's just an assembly job. A cup of meat sauce, a layer of noodles, more sauce, followed by a layer of cheese. Repeat until you have three layers and have used up all the ingredients.
  4. Bake until bubbly and you're ready to eat!
